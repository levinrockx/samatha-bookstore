About Samatha
Samatha – A Collective for Gender justice established in 2010 November 1 is a powerful initiative by a group of 29 dedicated women from all walks of life. It is the only publishing group of women in Kerala; perhaps in India too. Samatha has already published sixty Books. The major thrust areas of Samatha are
  • To undertake activities to promote awareness in gender justice.
  • To intervene in the political, economic, cultural, and social fields of interactions of the civil, social life upholding social/gender justice.
  • To institute chairs in honour of notable fellows who were catalyst to transform the social life of Kerala.
  • To promote subaltern studies with special reference to women’s power and creative aspects.
  • To conduct research on issues related to Gender and society and to engage in knowledge dissemination activities in the area of social/Gender justice.
  • To initiate a publication and production wing.
  • To develop a full- fledged Archives and Documentation centre for research activities.
  • To conduct awareness/consciousness/training programmes through various media.
  • To institute awards to eminent exponents for their contribution to the cause of gender justice
  • To act as a research/facilitating centre for the promotion of academic activities on gender justice.
Samatha is giving Monthly Pensions of Rs. 1000/- each to a political activist and a cultural activist (Women). The amount realized from the sale of the Books
